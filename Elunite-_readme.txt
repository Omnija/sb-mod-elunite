Elunite Race Mod

Author: Omnija
Assistant: Sotaeko

Description: 
Adds Elunite as a playable race.

Installation: 
Move the Elunite-.modpak into your Starbound/giraffe_storage/mods/ folder. 

Mod Dependency:
You need an Extended Race Selection mod for this to work.
